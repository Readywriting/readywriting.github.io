About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and educator with expertise in higher education, digital humanities, and inclusive pedagogy. My work focuses on transforming academic culture and promoting equity in educational spaces.

Currently, I am Assistant Director of Digital Learning at the Center for New Designs for Learning and Scholarship (CNDLS) at Georgetown University. I also serve as Editor of the National Teaching and Learning Forum, a leading publication in higher education pedagogy. My research interests include academic culture, digital pedagogy, and the intersection of technology and education.
